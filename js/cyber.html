Cyber Saathi – Women Empowerment through Cyber Security

A 15-day free course empowering women with essential cyber security awareness and skills
Initiative under CARE Connect's Women Empowerment Fund

Cyber Safety for Women in a Digital Age

This initiative aims to equip women (aged 25–45) with practical digital safety knowledge. Whether homemakers, entrepreneurs, or professionals – Cyber Saathi ensures that every woman can navigate the internet safely and confidently.

It includes mobile safety, fraud protection, social media privacy, and more with practical demonstrations.

Program Objectives

Cyber Awareness

Understanding scams, threats, identity theft, and cyber laws

Mobile Safety

Learn to protect smartphones and apps with smart practices

Social Media Privacy

Privacy settings and safe usage of Facebook, WhatsApp, etc.

Women-Specific Threats

Protection against stalking, sextortion, and harassment
