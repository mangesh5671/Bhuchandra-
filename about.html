Our Story

Bhuchandra Bahuudeshiya Shikshan Sanstha (BBSS) is a progressive organization committed to building an empowered and inclusive society through Education, Skill Development, Digital Literacy, and Sports. We believe in creating opportunities that inspire lifelong learning, self-growth, and community development.

Our Four Pillars

Education

Education is a powerful tool for transformation. BBSS provides open access to quality learning, supporting individuals on their academic journeys.

Skill Development

Practical skills unlock doors to opportunity. BBSS offers hands-on training programs in areas like tailoring, communication, technology, and entrepreneurship.

Digital Literacy

In today's digital world, having the right tech skills is essential. BBSS conducts digital literacy workshops and training to ensure everyone can navigate the digital space confidently.

Sports

Sports nurture discipline, teamwork, and leadership. Through structured training, recreational activities, and tournaments, BBSS promotes health, confidence, and active lifestyles.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create an empowered society where every child has access to quality education and life-changing opportunities.

Our Mission

To bridge the education gap for underprivileged communities by offering free courses, scholarships, and mentorship.

In Loving Memory

Shri Bhudeo Lahanuji Barde (9th October 1958 – 8th April 2014)

We humbly remember and honor Shri Bhudeo Lahanuji Barde, an inspiring soul, an engineer by profession, and a visionary by heart — born in the humble village of Hingani, Taluka Seloo, District Wardha.

In a time when opportunities were limited, he dared to dream big. His life's mission was clear and unwavering: to spread the light of education to every corner of society. His dedication to knowledge, discipline, and community upliftment laid the foundation for countless lives transformed through learning.

Though he is no longer with us, his values, principles, and vision continue to guide us. Bhuchandra Bahuudeshiya Shikshan Sanstha (BBSS) stands as a living tribute to his dream — to create a society where education, skill, and self-reliance empower all.

"Some lights never fade — they continue to guide us from beyond."

Message from the Founder

Dear Friends,

It gives me immense joy to welcome you to Bhuchandra Bahuudeshiya Shikshan Sanstha (BBSS) — a platform built on the values of inclusiveness, empowerment, and community growth.

Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully to society. Over the years, BBSS has evolved into a dynamic space that supports individuals through quality education, practical skills, digital awareness, and sports engagement — regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our initiative CARE Connect, under the Digital Literacy mission, is especially close to my heart. It is not just a program — it is a movement that brings people together through technology, ensuring timely access to knowledge, support, and opportunity.

At BBSS, we do not just run projects — we build futures. Every student, learner, sportsperson, and participant is a valuable part of this shared vision. We are deeply grateful for the support of our community, partners, and well-wishers who make this mission possible.

Let us continue to walk this path together — with hope in our hearts, purpose in our minds, and a commitment to creating a better tomorrow for all.

With warm regards,

Chandrakala Bhudeorao Barde
