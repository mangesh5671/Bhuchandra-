About Bhuchandra Foundation

Bhuchandra Bahuudeshiya Shikshan Sanstha (BBSS) is a progressive organization committed to building an empowered and inclusive society through Education, Skill Development, Digital Literacy, and Sports. We believe in creating opportunities that inspire lifelong learning, self-growth, and community development.

What We Do

School Support

We help students get access to quality education and basic learning resources.

Charity Drives

From food kits to clothes, we organize donation drives to support families in need.

Skill Workshops

We arrange workshops on life skills, coding, and spoken English for young learners.

Health Camps

Free check-ups and awareness camps to improve health and hygiene in rural areas.

Parent Counseling

We guide parents to better support their children's education and mental well-being.

Orphan Assistance

We extend care, education, and emotional support to orphaned and abandoned children.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Author: Yugant hedau

Date: 15 Jan 2025

Time: Fri 14:20

Description

In 1956, 2 years after death of Turing, John McCarthy organised a summer workshop to clarify and develop ideal about thinking machines and they choosing the name “Artificial Intelligence” for the project.
What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence notices to the qualification of computational systems to perform tasks ordinarily connected with human intelligence, such as learning, logicalness, mystery solving, etc.
How does AI work?
There are lots of works, which AI can do such as helping humans, playing games like chess, carrom, etc.Give answers of questions which humans ask to AI. Clear doubts.
Advantage of AI
There are various types of advantages of AI.

  • AI reducing human errors.
  • Helping humans 24/7.
  • Give answer of any question.
  • Telling the futuristic things to us.
  • Translate any complicated languages in meaningful languages that people can understand.
  • Behave friendly with us.

Disadvantages of AI
  • AI does not have emotions.
  • Lack of creativity.
  • Taking the human’s place in jobs.
  • They have security risks and misuse of AI.
  • Students don’t use their brain for thinking they ask questions to AI.

What is the 5g network:

×

Author:Tejaswini Karpe

Date: 17 Jan 2025

Time: Tue 16:30

Description

5G is the fifth generation of cellular network technology, offering faster speeds, lower frequency, and increased capacity compared to its predecessor, 4G. It enables new applications and experiences by supporting massive connectivity for devices and enabling real-time communication.
Benefits of 5G:
Increased Capacity:5G is designed to handle a much larger number of connected devices and a higher volume of data traffic.
Higher Speeds:5G provides significantly faster data transfer rates, with theoretical peak speeds reaching 20 Gbps, compared to 4G's 1 Gbps.s
Enhanced Reliability:5G provides a more reliable and consistent network connection, ensuring smooth performance for various applications.
Lower Frequency:5G offers ultra-low frequency, meaning minimal delay in data transmission, which is crucial for real-time application like remote surgery and autonomous vehicles.
